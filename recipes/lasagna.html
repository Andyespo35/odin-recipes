Lasagna

Lasagna is a classic dish that tastes incredible, but absolutely destroys my stomach.

You see, I have something that plagues 36% of Americans - I am extremely lactose intolerant. Like I actually broke a record for most lactose intolerant human that has ever been tested at the Children's Hospital of Philadelphia. What especially hurts is that I am Italian. I am forced to eat lasagna every year on Christmas eve before I eat fish. Every Christmas Eve I go to bed in agonizing pain. All that being said, Lasagna is incredible and you should read this so you can enjoy everything lasagna has to offer.

Ingredients

  • Sausage
  • Onion & Garlic
  • Sugar
  • Spices & Seasoning: fresh parsley, dried basil leaves, salt, italian seasoning, fennel seeds, and black pepper
  • Lasagna Noodles
  • Cheeses: Parmesan, mozzarella, and ricotta cheese
  • Egg
